In total 120 credits must be selected via any legitimate combination of modules. The yearlong
compulsory module CTEC3451 Development Project contains 30 credits in both term, which
contributes towards 15 credits in each term.

For both Computer Science and Software
Engineering students would have by default CTEC3451 Development Project and IMAT3423 Systems
Building: Methods. For Software Engineering students will additionally have the mandatory
module CTEC3902 Rigorous Systems. For Computer Science students would mean they would need to
select a further 30 credits of term 1 and 45 credits for term 2, Software Engineering students
requiring an additional 30 credits worth of modules to be chosen in both term 1 and term 2.
